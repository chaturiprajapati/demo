Overview

Founded initially as a real estate company in the year 1992 by a group of technocrats, the name changed from Meenakshi Construction Company to Meenakshi Infrastructures Pvt. Ltd in the year 2004. Ever since then, the Meenakshi Group has diversified into other core segments like Power Generation and Infrastructure. They have created over 12 million sq. ft. worth of properties in the IT destination of Hyderabad, and have built power projects in Arunachal Pradesh, and Andhra Pradesh. Power Generation, Infrastructure development and property development continue to be the crux of the group.

Our Vision

Meenakshi Group aims at being one of the world leaders in the fields of construction, infrastructure and power. Our vision is to provide cost effective, reliable and integrated services to our clients. We envision holistic growth in all different verticals that are a part of our system, staying ahead of the curve by keeping our parameters of quality and timeliness as our topmost priority.

Our Mission

To deliver services that surpass the expectations of our clients continues to be our mission statement. Be it during the construction of a new residential property, or during the construction of a new hydroelectric power project in collaboration with the government, we ensure customer and client satisfaction.
